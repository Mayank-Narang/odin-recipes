Dosa

Description

Made of Rice Batter can be easily made at home. Best served when stuffed with mashed potatoes, sambhar and coconut chutney

Ingredients

  • Rice Batter
  • Mashed potatoes
  • Coconut Chutney
  • Bit of Oil

Steps

  1. Using a small bowl spread rice batter on low flame heated pan in a circular direction
  2. Wait till the base becomes golder brown
  3. Add mashed potatoes and fold half dosa
  4. Serve with sambhar and chutney
