French Fries

Description

Simple yet tasty dish prepared from potatoes. It can be served plain with salt and pepper. Some people like to eat with tomato ketchup

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Sauce
  • Knife

Steps

  1. Wash, peel and cut potatoes to apt size
  2. Deep fry it in oil of your choice
  3. Fry until golden brown
  4. Sprinkle salt, pepper, etc.
  5. Serve hot with tomato ketchup
