Papdi Chaat

Description

More like an assembly of different stuff rendering a dish which is enough to make mouth water of a child or an adult.

Ingredients

  • Papdi
  • Dahi
  • Lal Chutney
  • Green Chutney
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Place papdi in a plate
  2. Add dahi on top
  3. Add lal chutney
  4. Sprinkle salt and pepper and serve
